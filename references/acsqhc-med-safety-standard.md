# ACSQHC — Medication Safety Standard (NSQHS)

- **URL:** https://www.safetyandquality.gov.au/national-standards/nsqhs-standards/medication-safety-standard
- **Publisher:** Australian Commission on Safety and Quality in Health Care
- **Accessed:** 2026-06-22
- **Tier:** 1 (National standards body)
- **Summary:** The Medication Safety Standard (Action 4.10) requires health service organisations to implement medication reconciliation at admission, transfer, and discharge. Specifies that a BPMH must be recorded when commencing an episode of care.
