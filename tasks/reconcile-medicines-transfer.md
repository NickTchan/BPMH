# Reconcile Medicines at Transfer of Care

## Task Description

The process of comparing a patient's Best Possible Medication History against their current medication orders at every transfer of care (admission, intra-hospital transfer, discharge), identifying discrepancies, and resolving them in collaboration with the treating team.

## Task Boundary

**Starts when:** A BPMH has been obtained and verified, and the patient is being transferred between care settings or teams.

**Ends when:** All discrepancies have been identified, classified (intentional vs. unintentional), resolved or documented with rationale, and the medication orders accurately reflect the intended treatment plan.
